About Me

Hi, my name is Bohan Cheng. I am a game developer and I have a game programming advanced diploma. I am very passionate about making games and programming in general. I started making games since high school. I made a lot of projects throughout the years and learnt a lot of things. I am able work independently and efficiently because I self-tough most of the knowledge about programming and how to use a game engine like Unity and Unreal. However, I have been working in teams i n my college years . I know how to work as a team and how to get well with team members. Most importantly, I as well know how to communicate with other members friendly and efficiently. I am also a fast-learner, I can adapt new things and start working on it quickly. For instance, when I first used Unreal Engine 4. There was very little documents and resources about it, especially about UE4 C++ programming. But I did manage to learn it in short amount of time because I navigate around engine, explore and figure out how things work. I like to challenge myself from time to time, I want to get better and better for the things I love. Which means I learn things continuously as the technology side of things are growing.
